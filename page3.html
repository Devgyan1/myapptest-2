ASIAGO TOASTS RECIPE

INGREDIENTS
1/3 cup Hellmann's® or Best Foods® Mayonnaise Dressing with Olive Oil 1 cup shredded Asiago cheese or Parmesan-Reggiano cheese 1 clove garlic, finely chopped 1 Tbsp. chopped fresh oregano 1 Tbsp. chopped flat-leaf parsley 1/2 French baguette, cut into 18 slices



1
COMBINE all ingredients except baguette in small bowl. 2
ARRANGE baguette slices in a single layer on baking sheet; spread cheese mixture on baguette. Broil until the cheese is melted and golden, about 3 minutes.
